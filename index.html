Athena des Myron

Athena is the goddess of wisdom in ancient Greek mythology, and is considered the goddess of war, craftsmanship and strategy. She is the figure of wisdom, skill, and strategy, and her wit makes her a concern in military and political strategy. Similar to product management, Athena was a decision maker, innovator and leader. She led the Greek army to victory in battle and also innovated in the field of handicrafts, such as pioneering the method of weaving cloth.
